Money Bank

Are you ready to play a Match 3 game? Money Bank gives you 8 opportunities to reveal the money values for each ticket. Try to get 3 identical money values to win the associated prize. If you get 3 to 5 gold bars, you win up to 3 additional prizes immediately.

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the minus (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the plus (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

[top]

Auto Play

Press AUTO PLAY to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until 8 selections have been made.

[top]

How To Play

The object of Money Bank is to find 3 cash counters with the same money amount. Find 3 to 5 gold bars win up to 3 additional prizes.

At the start of the game, Money Bank displays 12 cash counters. Selecting a cash counter starts to count money and show the money value, and using AUTO PLAY has the game play to completion automatically.

If the player finds:

  • 3 cash counters with the same money amount, he or she wins the corresponding prize.
    •
  • 3, 4, or 5 gold bars, he or she wins 1, 2, or 3 additional prizes.
    •

To complete play, the player must select 8 cash counters or use AUTO PLAY, after which a message appears indicating the game’s outcome, and play ends.

[top]

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
